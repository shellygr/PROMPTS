=== /plan rubinstein ===
In this folder what you see is phone-based 'scans' of a very special book - the programme for the 1st Arthur Rubinstein Piano Masters competition from 1974!
It is very rare.
The first copy I got was already unwrapped with pages disconnected. 
Then, I found out there are missing pages, so we took the second copy which was not torn up in the cover and took pictures of pages that were missing.
To make things worse, the booklet does not have page numbering!
so while I am confident that I took sufficient pictures of everything, we have a few issues in organizing the pictures:
1. Making sure all are rotated in the same way
2. getting rid of fingers that were used to hold the pages straight
3. fixing up curvature of pages in the good booklet and straightening the text
4. fixing up glare effects, since the pages are chromo.
5. properly number the pictures according to where they are chronologically. I took pictures of the existing pages in the torn-up booklet first and then went backwards with the second booklet.
6. split pages since I took sometimes picture of both sides
7. pick best retouched pages and use the consistent numbering

After rotation, do not modify original pics. Figure out which images are best for retouching, make sure the order is correct, and then perform the retouches. write all retouched images into a new folder "retouched/".
